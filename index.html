关于我们About Us

Cecily Dance Company 是一支跨文化舞团，擅长将传统舞蹈元素与当代编舞语言融合，呈现具有视觉张力与情感厚度的舞台作品。 Cecily Dance Company blends traditional forms with contemporary choreography to create visually striking and emotionally resonant stage works.

我们的使命Our Mission

通过跨文化合作与教育项目，推动公众对舞蹈的欣赏与参与。 To engage audiences through cross-cultural collaborations and educational programmes.
